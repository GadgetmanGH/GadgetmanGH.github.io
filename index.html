About

TiguerSound is a mixing and mastering studio run by audio engineer Dave Tigue

Dave has been mixing, mastering, and producing for over 25 years.
Operating from his home studio, he specializes in remote collaboration with artists and musicians. Dave brings a wealth of practical knowledge and expertise in hardware, software, multiple digital audio workstations, and live sound.
His impressive portfolio includes work on award-winning films and commercials, as well as years of audio editing for championship winning dance and figure skating competitors. Dave has collaborated with numerous artists to bring their songs from idea to reality, mixing and mastering hundreds of tracks throughout his career.
